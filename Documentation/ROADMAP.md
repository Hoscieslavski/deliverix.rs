# Strategic Roadmap: Deliverix

This document outlines the product roadmap and expansion phases for **Deliverix**, divided into short-term, medium-term, and long-term milestones.

---

```
Phase 1: Stabilization (Completed) ➔ Phase 2: SEO Growth (Short-Term) ➔ Phase 3: Platform Integration (Medium-Term) ➔ Phase 4: Full Ecosystem (Long-Term)
```

---

## 1. Short-Term Focus: Organic Scaling & Conversion

**Timeline:** Next 1 - 3 Months  
**Goal:** Maximize high-intent organic traffic and optimize conversion rates.

### 1.1 Content Factory & SEO Seeding
- Publish 15 deep, high-intent articles addressing candidate FAQs (e.g., *"Kolika je satnica Glovo dostavljača"*, *"Kako izabrati najbolju partnersku agenciju"*).
- Target regional sub-terms in Serbia to dominate search rankings outside Beograd (e.g., Novi Sad, Niš, Kragujevac, Pančevo).

### 1.2 UX Fine-Tuning
- Further minimize candidate registration steps on mobile.
- Implement an automated SMS or WhatsApp confirmation sequence upon registration.

### 1.3 Partner Integrations
- Secure agreements with 5 leading fleets across Serbia.
- Standardize the lead delivery mechanism, reducing the latency from registration to fleet contact.

---

## 2. Medium-Term Focus: Interactive Portals & CRM Automation

**Timeline:** Next 3 - 6 Months  
**Goal:** Automate candidate-partner matching and build interactive customer interfaces.

### 2.1 Independent Partner Portal
- Build a dedicated, secure dashboard for fleet partners.
- Partners can log in, view candidate cards assigned to them, update onboarding statuses (e.g., from `SENT_TO_PARTNER` to `ACTIVE`), and upload contract approvals.

### 2.2 Interactive Candidate Portal
- Launch a lightweight web application for candidates.
- Candidates can log in to track their application status, download onboarding training guides, and receive recommendations for partner fleets.

### 2.3 Automated Referral Engine
- Implement a built-in referral link manager allowing active couriers to invite friends.
- Track conversions and calculate payout rewards dynamically.

---

## 3. Long-Term Focus: Full On-Demand Recruitment Ecosystem

**Timeline:** Next 6 - 12 Months  
**Goal:** Establish Deliverix as the premier recruitment and support ecosystem for gig economy workers in South-East Europe.

### 3.1 Deliverix Mobile Application
- Build a companion mobile application (iOS & Android) focusing on:
  - Real-time application tracking.
  - Vetted courier news and earnings calculators.
  - Active chat channels with fleet operators.
  - Specialized discount vouchers (gas, vehicle servicing, equipment).

### 3.2 Advanced Analytics & AI Matching
- Implement ML-driven matching algorithms to pair candidates with the most suitable fleet partner based on their vehicle choice, location, and past conversion history.
- Dynamic earnings forecaster predicting earnings based on current platform demand.

### 3.3 Expansion to Neighboring Markets
- Localize the Deliverix platform to launch in Croatia, Bosnia & Herzegovina, and Montenegro.
- Replicate the proven pre-rendered SEO engine and CRM pipeline to capture regional traffic.
